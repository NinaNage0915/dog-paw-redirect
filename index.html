Protect Your Dog's Paws

The pavement can be much hotter than the air temperature, causing painful burns and blisters in just minutes. In fact, asphalt and sidewalks can be 30°-60° hotter than the air. During warm weather, walk your dog during cooler parts of the day and test the pavement with your hand before heading out.
